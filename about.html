The birth of a dream

Scientist and entrepreneur Dr. Norman Osborn established Osborn Pharmaceutical Laboratories with the vision to make medicines accessible to everyone.
At OPL, we are constantly building and investing in cutting-edge technologies to fast-forward the pace of our science, helping us expand and accelerate our drug discovery and push the boundaries of science to achieve the next wave of breakthroughs.
Dedicated to Discovery and Committed to Cure has always been and continues to remain, our guiding purpose.
Driven by the same purpose, we have extended our presence to 200+ countries providing over 2,500 products across various therapeutic categories in 80+ dosage forms.
